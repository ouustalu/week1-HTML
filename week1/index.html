The Room (2003)

A seriously questionable cult movie.

The movie [0/10], the experience [10/10] — a true cinematic paradox!

Overview

Johnny is a successful bank executive who lives quietly in a San Francisco townhouse with his fiancee, Lisa.

One day, setting aside any scruples, Lisa seduces Johnny's best friend, Mark. From there, nothing will be the same again.

From legendary and unreasonable one-liners to a memorable and a bit scary scene with Tommy Wiseau and his fiancee's red dress, this movie will baffle you with it's plotline, obscure pacing and purely masterful actmanship.

The initial box office grossed out at an amazing $1900 but later on, over the course of 20 years, this masterpiece made a fivefold profit!

Budget $6 million
Revenue $30 million
